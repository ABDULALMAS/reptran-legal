User Data Deletion — RepTran

At RepTran, you have full control of your data. If you wish to delete your account and all associated data obtained via Facebook Login (or any other sign-in method), you can request deletion at any time.

How to request data deletion

  1. Log in to your RepTran account → Go to Settings → Delete Account and follow the instructions.
  2. Alternatively, you can send us an email at support@reptran.com with the subject Delete My Account.

Once we receive your request, we will permanently delete your personal data (including name, email, and any data retrieved from Facebook/Google/Twitter sign-in) within a reasonable time.

For more details, please review our Privacy Policy.
